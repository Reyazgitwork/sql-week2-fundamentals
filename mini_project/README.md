## 🟣 Mini Project – E-commerce SQL Analysis (MySQL)

### Objective
Built a small relational e-commerce database and answered real business questions using **JOINs, GROUP BY, CTEs, and window functions**.

### Tables Created
- `customers`
- `products`
- `orders`
- `order_items`

### Analysis Performed
- Total Revenue
- Revenue by Product
- Best-selling Products (Units Sold)
- Monthly Revenue Trend
- Top Customers by Spending
- Customers Spending Above Average (CTE)
- Product Revenue Ranking (DENSE_RANK)

### Key Results
- **Total Revenue:** **$11,120**
- **Top Revenue Products:** **Laptop** and **Phone** (tied for Rank #1)
- **Highest Revenue Month:** **2024-01**

### Business Insights
- Revenue is concentrated in **high-priced Electronics**, especially **Laptop** and **Phone**.
- **January 2024** generated the highest revenue, suggesting a strong early-year spike.
- Product ranking shows a tie at the top, which highlights multiple strong revenue drivers rather than dependency on a single product.
